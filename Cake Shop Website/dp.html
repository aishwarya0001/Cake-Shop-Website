Our Story

Gigi’s has been swirling cupcakes for more than 12 years, growing from the first location in Nashville to more than 50 locations in 21 states. While cupcakes are at the heart of our kitchens, we’re big fans of other sweet treats, too: cookies, brownies and donuts bring a little variety to the party. Each Gigi’s is locally owned and operated, bringing their local community into each bakery. A freshly baked app makes online ordering easy online ordering for take-out and delivery, with rewards for loyal fans.
